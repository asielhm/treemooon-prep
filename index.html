Reliable preparation and logistics for your inventory

Ask Another Question
What is your standard processing time?

Our standard processing time is 24–48 business hours. Larger or more complex projects may require additional time.

Do you receive freight from commercial carriers?

Yes. We receive cartons, pallets and commercial freight shipments from transportation and logistics companies.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments can be made by credit card, bank transfer or Zelle.

Can you prepare products for Amazon FBA?

Yes. We provide FNSKU and UPC labeling, suffocation warning labels, polybagging, inspection, repacking and shipment preparation.

Do you offer monthly pallet storage?

Yes. Monthly pallet storage is available at $25.00 USD per pallet, subject to space availability and service terms.

Are packaging materials included in every rate?

Large secondary shipping cartons, extra wooden pallets and other materials not supplied by the customer may be billed separately at current market cost.
